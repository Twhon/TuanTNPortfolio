Bio
"Whether it's CFD or a watch movement — understand the physics, make it work, make it right."

Full-time engineering student with a focus on aerodynamics and performance systems. As lead aerodynamicist for Dallas Formula Racing I directed design and testing of full aero packages for FSAE competition from the ground up.

Interests & Skills

Outside of school and racing I design and build watches — a craft I've turned into a small business. I'm drawn to projects that demand both rigorous analysis and hands-on making.

Core competencies: aerodynamics, CFD simulation, CAD, fabrication, electronics design, high-voltage systems, and horology.
